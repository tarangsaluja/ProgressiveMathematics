The Philosophy of Mathematics

Submission Guidelines

Essays submitted to this page are about the philosophy of mathematics. Relevant topics include different philosophical frameworks which can be used to understand concepts in mathematics, critical perspectives on mathematics, episteomological discussions on mathematical knowledge, logic and computation, and broadly anything which fits in the category of philosophy.

All articles should include required background knowledge at the top.

To submit an essay, please email phil_math_roses@gmail.com.
