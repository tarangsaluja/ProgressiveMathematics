Insightful Problems

Submission Guidelines

Essays submitted to this page are about mathematical problems which have some delightful kernel of knowledge or beauty to them. These are problems which open one's perspective to something insightful, thoughtful, and beautiful about mathematics. The essays should clearly state the problem and then present the solution and commentary in a way which illuminates what makes this problem wonderful.

All articles should include required background knowledge at the top.

To submit an essay, please email pe_math_roses@gmail.com.
