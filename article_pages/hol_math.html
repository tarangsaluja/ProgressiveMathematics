Holistic Mathematical Essays

Submission Guidelines

Essays submitted to this page should provide an alternative perspective of mathematical concepts, histories, or topics. These essays should integrate mathematics with, or understaind mathematics in the context of, different kinds of study, such as music, art, social sciences, and more.

All articles should include required background knowledge at the top.

To submit an essay, please email hol_math_roses@gmail.com.
