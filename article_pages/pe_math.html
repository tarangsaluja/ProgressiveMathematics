The Political Economy of Mathematics

Submission Guidelines

Essays submitted to this page are about mathematics and political economy. In particular, these essays will contextualize mathematics as inextricably linked with the political and economic systems which give it a social form. Relevant topics can include the political and historic factors which affect mathematics education, the control of mathematical knowledge by governments and corporations, the use of mathematics for national defense, surveillance, and war, the differentiated access to mathematics and technology among different segments of the global populaton, and more.

All articles should include required background knowledge at the top.

To submit an essay, please email pe_math_roses@gmail.com.
